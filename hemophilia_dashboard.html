Methodology

Priority Score Calculation:

  • Need Score (60% weight): Based on Factor VIII per capita - lower availability indicates higher need
  • Low Aid Score (25% weight): Based on current humanitarian aid levels - lower aid indicates higher priority
  • Low DR Score (15% weight): Based on DR donation values - lower donations indicate higher priority

Need Categories (based on Factor VIII per capita):

  • Far Below Min. Survival (0-0.5): Critical need - far below minimum survival threshold
  • Below Min. Survival (0.5-1): High need - below minimum survival threshold
  • Moderate Need (1-3): Moderate need level
  • Low Need (>3): Adequate availability
